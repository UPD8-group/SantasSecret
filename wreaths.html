Caring for Your Handcrafted Wreath

Keep your beautiful wreath looking fresh and vibrant throughout the season with these simple care tips

Moisture Management

Lightly mist fresh wreaths every few days. Avoid overwatering dried arrangements. Keep away from direct heat sources.

Light Protection

Display in areas with indirect sunlight to prevent fading. Rotate occasionally for even aging of natural materials.

Gentle Cleaning

Use a soft brush to remove dust. For deeper cleaning, use a hair dryer on cool setting to blow away debris.
