Christmas Fun Facts

Candy Canes

The average candy cane is about 13 cm tall. Aussies love a sweet treat—especially at Christmas! While exact numbers aren't tracked locally, candy canes are one of the top-selling seasonal lollies across Australia every December.

Christmas Trees

It takes around 7–10 years for a Christmas tree to reach full height (about 2 metres). In Australia, over 1.4 million real Christmas trees are sold each year, with demand steadily rising thanks to a shift toward more sustainable, natural options.

Santa's Aussie Journey

To reach all the children across Australia's 7.7 million km², Santa would have to fly faster than a jet—zipping between bushland, beaches, cities, and outback towns. That's a whole lot of magic and maybe even a stop for pavlova!

Gift Giving

The tradition of gift giving comes from the story of the Three Wise Men—but in Australia, it's also about backyard cricket, family BBQs, and secret Santa swaps under the gum tree.
