The Founder's Story

"Putting Pain in a Box"

"I used to think the idea was ridiculous... until I tried it."

Santa's Secret was born from a personal journey. For Kim, decorating became a ritual of healing during a battle with autoimmune disease. It was a way to focus entirely on joy.

Every bauble and wreath in this shop is curated not just for its beauty, but for its power to spark that same healing light in your home.
