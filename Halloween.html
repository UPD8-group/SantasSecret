Regional Recognition

A Model for
Community Events

What started as a simple idea — invite families into the Bungendore business district for a free trick-or-treat event — turned into something much bigger. The event was so well received that it has now been recognised and encouraged by Regional Development Australia Southern NSW & ACT as a model for community engagement across the region.

A completely free event that brought hundreds of families together, supported local businesses, and strengthened the fabric of our town. This is what community looks like.

"A free event that invited families into the Bungendore business district to trick or treat — encouraging families to visit their local businesses — is now being encouraged by Regional Development Australia Southern NSW & ACT."

— Kim Barlow, Santa's Secret

Community Event Recap

Bungendore's Halloween
Trick or Treat

On Friday, October 31st 2025, the streets of Bungendore came alive. Local businesses threw open their doors and families poured in — it was exactly the kind of evening that makes this town so special.

Friday, October 31st 2025

The Whole Town Got Involved

Multiple local Bungendore businesses opened their doors for trick or treating, creating a trail of Halloween magic right through the heart of town. Families with kids, babies, pets and all — everyone was welcome, and it was completely free.
