◆ The Programme ◆

What Awaits You

Three reasons to put on the costume and come into town.

Trick-or-Treat The District

Wander through the local business district collecting treats from participating shops. A spooky stroll through the heart of Bungendore.

Meet The Addams Family

Step inside a vintage 1934 Packard limousine for a free family photo experience. Mysterious, kooky, altogether ooky.

Costume Prizes

Come as your favourite character — the more unconventional, the better. Prizes to be won across all ages.
